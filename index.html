Overview
The DUVA Lucero Mini is a compact, surface-mounted LED downlight tailored for residential and general indoor applications. Designed with a minimal aesthetic and a precision reflector, it delivers efficient beam control and soft, uniform illumination. Constructed from durable aluminum and finished with anti-rust powder coating, it ensures long-term performance with a clean cylindrical profile. Tool-free ceiling installation and optional reflector finishes make Lucero Mini ideal for corridors, retail, and hospitality spaces.
